Watches are timekeeping devices that are typically worn on the wrist or carried in a pocket. They provide a convenient and portable way to tell the time. Watches can be analog, with hour and minute hands, or digital, displaying the time numerically. Some watches also include additional features such as date displays, alarms, timers, and chronographs for measuring elapsed time. They come in various styles, materials, and brands, catering to different preferences and fashion trends.

Smart Band

Smart bands or smart bracelets are quickly displacing the traditional wristwatch. The reason is simple, if you have a pocket-friendly band that tells you the time along with keeping track of your fitness, then you would opt for it too. Most smart bands are controlled by a dedicated application on your phone. This application keeps track of your daily activities such as the number of steps walked in a day, heart rate and more.
